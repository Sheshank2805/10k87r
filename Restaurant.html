WELCOME TO PLATFORM65

We bring to you the latest restaurant trend: A restaurant that serves food on toy trains with no waiters. Yes, you read that correctly: at Platform 65, you simply place your order and have your delicious food delivered to your table by a toy train. Not only that, but the entire restaurant ambience is designed as a train, from seats similar to the Tejas Express to fans similar to those seen in the trains. The Ambience at Platform 65 will set you in a nostalgic mood and ensures you feel like you are actually traveling on a train.
